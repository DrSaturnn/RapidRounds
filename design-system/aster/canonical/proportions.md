# Aster Proportions

This document records the locked proportions that the future rigged Aster model
must preserve.

## Canonical Reference

Use `design-system/aster/canonical/neutral.png` as the visual reference for all
measurements and proportion checks.

## Locked Proportion Priorities

1. Large rounded helmet/head with compact body underneath.
2. Wide glossy black visor occupying most of the face area.
3. Warm oval LED eyes with stable spacing and glow.
4. Small rounded torso with centered glowing chest crystal.
5. Very short stubby arms with rounded segmented hands.
6. Short rounded legs/feet tucked under the body.
7. Floating posture with soft amber hover shadow.

## Orthographic Requirement

Orthographic renders must behave like a physical model rotation:

- `Front.png`
- `ThreeQuarterFront.png`
- `Side.png`
- `ThreeQuarterBack.png`
- `Back.png`
- `Top.png`

The model may rotate. The character identity may not drift.
