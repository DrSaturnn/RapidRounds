# Aster Materials

## Ceramic Shell

The shell should read as high-quality warm white ceramic, not flat white plastic.
Use layered highlights, subtle seams, and low-contrast panel borders.

## Visor

The faceplate should read as deep glossy black glass. It should remain large,
rounded, and continuous.

## Eyes

Eyes are warm amber LED forms. They should be small, softly glowing, and
expression-bearing. Emotion comes from eye shape and posture, not a mouth.

## Metallic Accents

Side accents and small details should read as champagne anodized aluminum or
soft gold metal.

## Core

The chest crystal is Aster's energy source. It glows softly in warm amber and
must remain visible at every meaningful scale.
