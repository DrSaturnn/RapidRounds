# Aster Animation Guidelines

Motion should feel alive but quiet.

Allowed:

- gentle hover
- slow blink
- subtle chest glow
- slight thinking tilt
- tiny celebratory sparkle
- map or teaching accessory appearing briefly

Avoid:

- exaggerated bouncing
- squash and stretch
- rapid shaking
- large celebratory motion
- motion that draws attention away from clinical reasoning

All Aster animations MUST respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
